The Persistence of Memory

**Artist:** Salvador Dalí

**Year of Creation:** 1931

Short Description

This surrealist masterpiece is one of Salvador Dalí's most recognizable works. Known for its dream-like landscape and melting clocks draped over a barren landscape, it explores the themes of time, decay, and the subconscious mind. It is a key piece in the Surrealism movement.
