Mona Lisa

**Artist:** Leonardo Di Vinci

**Year of Creation:** 1503

Short Description

La Gioconda, or Mona Lisa, is a portrait half-length painting by the Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci. It is considered an archetypal masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance and has been described as "the best known, the most visited, the most written about, the most sung about, the most parodied work of art in the world.
